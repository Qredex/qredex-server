# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, constructive, and professional.

Expected behavior:

- communicate clearly and respectfully
- assume good intent
- focus feedback on the code, docs, or behavior
- help keep the project welcoming for contributors and users

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, abuse, or discrimination
- doxxing or sharing private information
- bad-faith disruption, intimidation, or threats

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or participants that violate this standard.

For conduct concerns, contact:

- `os@qredex.com`
